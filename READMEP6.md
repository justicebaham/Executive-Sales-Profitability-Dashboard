**Project 6: Executive Sales \& Profitability Dashboard**

**Project Overview:** An analysis using a BigQuery SQL Query to engineer KPIs, then creating a dynamic interactive Executive Dashboard by using an end-to-end data pipeline.



**Problem:**

The business lacked  real-time visibility of KPIs, which made it difficult to compare regional performance quickly.



**Action:**

* Data Engineering: Engineered a Logical View in BigQuery using SUM and SAFE\_DIVIDE functions to calculate row-level KPIs and ensure dashboard stability.
* BI Development: Developed a Business Intelligence (BI) solution by connecting the BigQuery view to Looker Studio, establishing an end-to-end data pipeline.
* UI/UX Optimization: Designed an executive-style interface featuring KPI Scorecards with compact number formatting and an Interactive Region Slicer to facilitate rapid cross-regional comparisons.



**Result:**

* **The Bottom Line:** The total sales were $2.3M and the total profit was $286.4K.
* **Profitability Benchmark:** The overall profit margin percentage was 12.03%.
* **Category Leader:** The category that drove the most sales was Technology with a revenue total of $836,154.03.
* **Segment Insight:** The customer segment that was most profitable was the consumer segment which accounted for 46.8% of profits.











**Tools Used:**

* SQL (BigQuery): Data extraction and aggregation.
* Data Visualization (Looker Studio): Executive Dashboard creation.
